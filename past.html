Tiny Bunker

Tiny Bunker Production Log

Tiny Bunker was a project where I had to decorate a room with various assets which I had made myself, this project was a collective project where upwards of 40 people each created a room and the doors to either side of room would connect these rooms together, for my room I went with a medieval/fantasy theme. I created all of the models as well as a few of the textures myself, the other textures were sourced online, I also created the animation for the chest myself. The button aboves links to the full production log I made for the assignment.
